Ticket Office Handbook

Subscriptions

Overview

The Oregon Symphony offers a variety of subscription ticket packages. There are two types: Fixed Series package and a Flex Series package. The Fixed Series packages are a specific set of pre-selected concerts. The Flex Series can be mostly customized by the subscriber.

The benefits of being a Fixed Series subscriber are:

  1. Lowest Prices for Best Seats
  2. Subscriber Discounts on Add-On Concerts
  3. Free Exchanges
  4. No Handling Fees on Add-On Concerts
  5. Invites to Special Subscriber Only Events
  6. The Same Seat Each Season
  7. Early Access to Special Concerts
  8. Rehearsal Passes
  9. Payment Plan Options

The benefits of being a Flex Series subscriber are:

  1. Subscriber Discounts on Add-On Concerts
  2. Free Exchanges
  3. No Handling Fees on Add-On Concerts
  4. Invites to Special Subscriber Only Events
  5. Ability to Create a Custom Package of Their Choosing

Fixed Series

Classical Series

Classical concerts are concerts where classical music is the focus. We offer 18 different Classical concerts. They can be purchased in sets of 6, 12, or all 18. The Classical concerts take place on Saturdays at 7:30 pm, Sundays at 2:00 pm, and Mondays at 7:30 pm.

Pops Series

Pops concerts are concerts where popular music is the focus. We offer 4 different Pops concerts. The Pops concerts take place on Saturdays at 7:30 pm and Sunday at 2:00 pm.

Popcorn Series

Popcorn concerts are movies that we screen while the Orchestra plays the score live. We offer 4 Popcorn Package concerts. The Popcorn concerts take place on Saturdays at 7:30 pm and Sundays at 2:00pm. Sometimes, due to popularity, there may be an added show on Friday Night or another weekend day.

Kids Series

Kids concerts are hour long concerts geared towards children under 7. We offer 3 Kids concerts. The Kids concerts take place on Sundays at 2:00 pm.

Salem Series Concerts

Salem Series concerts are 6 select Classical Concerts performed by the Oregon Symphony Orchestra in Salem, OR. We offer 6 Salem Series Concerts. Salem concerts take place on Fridays at 7:30 pm at Smith Auditorium at Willamette University.

Flex Package

Choose Your Own (CYO)

The Choose Your Own (CYO) Package is a flexible package made of a minimum of 3 eligible concerts. Currently, the only eligible CYO concerts are all 18 Classical and the 4 Pops concerts. CYO subscribers can add Kids, Popcorn, or Special concerts to their package at full price.

Special Concerts

We offer a variety of one-night-only Special concerts. They can range from famous classical musicians, Broadway singers, or popular music acts. Special concerts can happen any day of the week but are generally on weeknights at 7:30 pm. You receive no benefits for purchasing Special concerts.

Premium Special Concerts

Each season we have a small number of Premium Special concerts that we do not discount even for our Subscribers. This season Itzhak Perlman and the Pink Martini Valentine concerts are the Premium Special concerts. Premium Special concerts can happen any day of the week.
